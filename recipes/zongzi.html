Zongzi

Description

Zongzi, or 粽子 in Chinese, is an important part of Chinese cuisine, and we will teach you how to make some delicious zongzi today.

There are two main variations of zongzi found in China, sweet zongzi, and salty/savoury zongzi. For this recipe, we will focus on the salty version as it is the superior of the two.

Ingredients

  • sticky rice
  • salt
  • cold water
  • pork, beef or any ground meat of your choice
  • zongzi leaves

Steps

  1. Wash all ingredients
  2. Add sald and meat into leaves
  3. Wrap leaves carefully
  4. Boil zongzi in water
