Dumplings

Description

When it comes to both taste and versatility, there are a few dishes that match that of the dumpling. The juicy morsels of filling encased in dough are an all-in-one meal. They're an everyday food, enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner.

While it's easy to find a bags of frozen dumplings at the grocery store, they're no match for the homemade kind. With this recipe, which includes a homemade dumpling dough and ground meat and vegetable filling, and some practice, you'll be folding your own like a pro in no time.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • salt
  • cold water
  • pork, beef or any ground meat of your choice

Steps

  1. Turn flour into dough
  2. Smash dough hard and make it into dumpling skin
  3. Add sald and meat into skin
  4. Boil dumplings in water
