Description

Mapo tofu is a popular Chinese dish from Sichuan province. It consists of tofu set in a spicy sauce, typically a thin, oily, and bright red suspension, based on douban, and douchi, along with minced meat, traditionally beef.

As someone very familiar in Sichuan cuisine, today I will show you how to make mapo tofu.

Ingredients

  • oil
  • chilli peppers
  • red chilli
  • pork
  • tofu

Steps

  1. Toast the chillies
  2. Heat oil
  3. Add everything into pot
  4. Cook everything
